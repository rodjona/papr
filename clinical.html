Clinical Education

Simulation laboratory and clinical experience.

Students begin clinical practicum on the first day of the program and participate in supervised clinical training.

Simulation Laboratory Section

Students will participate in simulation-based training including:

  • Air Embolism
  • Oxygenator Failure
  • Massive Hemorrhage
  • Low Venous Return
  • Pump Failure
  • ECMO Emergencies
  • Emergency Change out procedures

Clinical Experience

The clinical practicum starts on the first day of the program. Students must perform a minimum of 75 adult clinical bypass procedures, 20 Autotransfusion cases, 5 ECMO simulations, 5 Plasmapheresis Procedures, and 10 TAVR observations.

Students will be assigned a clinical instructor who will assist and monitor them throughout the cases in the clinical practicum.
