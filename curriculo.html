Curriculum

Three semesters of core perfusion education and clinical practice.

The program follows a set curriculum. Each stage must be successfully completed before advancing to the next.

Academic Calendar

Simulation Laboratory Section

Students will participate in simulation-based training including:

Simulation-Based Training

  • Air Embolism
  • Oxygenator Failure
  • Massive Hemorrhage
  • Low Venous Return
  • Pump Failure
  • ECMO Emergencies
  • Emergency Change out procedures

Clinical Experience

The clinical practicum starts on the first day of the program. You must perform a minimum of 75 adult clinical bypass procedures, 20 Autotransfusion cases, 5 ECMO simulations, 5 Plasmapheresis Procedures, and 10 TAVR observations. You will be assigned a clinical instructor who will assist and monitor you throughout the cases in the clinical practicum.
