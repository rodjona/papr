Admissions

Requirements, tuition, and application documents.

Applicants must hold a baccalaureate degree from an accredited college or university. Students with pre-medical studies may be evaluated for admission.

Required Courses

  • 8 hours Chemistry with lab
  • 8 hours Biology with lab
  • 6 hours Human Anatomy and Physiology
  • 3 hours Physics
  • 3 hours College Algebra or higher-level math
  • 3 hours Statistics

Tuition and Fees

Tuition is $50,000, payable in 4 installments.

  • $25,000 due at registration
  • $8,333 after sixth month
  • $8,333 after twelfth month
  • $8,333 after eighteenth month and before graduation
