Curriculum

Three semesters of didactic, laboratory, simulation, and clinical training.

The curriculum is rigorous and sequenced. Students must successfully complete each stage before advancing to the next.

First Semester

Cardiac Anatomy and Physiology (3)
Cardiology (2)
Basic Surgery and Monitoring (2)
Perfusion Devices w/Lab I (2)
Perfusion Technology I (3)
Clinical Practicum I (12)

Total: 24 credits

Second Semester

Blood Management (3)
Cardiovascular Pharmacology (3)
Perfusion Devices w/Lab II (2)
Perfusion Technology II (3)
Clinical Practicum II (12)

Total: 23 credits

Third Semester

ECMO Fundamentals (2)
Plasmapheresis (2)
Cardiovascular Pathology (3)
Research Methodology (1)
Physiological Management of Bypass (2)
Clinical Practicum III (12)

Total: 22 credits
