About the Program

18-month post-baccalaureate certificate in perfusion technology.

The program trains students to use the cardiopulmonary bypass machine and other equipment associated with open-heart surgery, autotransfusion, plasma exchange, and ECMO.

Program Design

Academic courses and clinical rotations are held concurrently. Lectures are taught by PRPA staff, clinical affiliate surgeons, anesthesiologists, perfusionists, and guest lecturers.

Schedules may change based on hospital surgical needs. Clinical responsibilities may require evenings, nights, weekends, holidays, and call segments.

Duration

18 months across three rigorous semesters.

Clinical Focus

Open-heart surgery, cardiopulmonary bypass, ECMO, blood management, and plasma exchange.

Simulation

Scenario-based emergency response training.

Outcome

Certificate of cardiovascular perfusion upon meeting all requirements.
