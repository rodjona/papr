Admissions & Requirements

Requirements, tuition, and enrollment details.

Applicants must have a baccalaureate degree from an accredited college or university. Pre-medical studies may be evaluated for admission.

Tuition & Fees

Total tuition: $50,000, payable in four installments.

  • $25,000 due at registration.
  • $8,333 after the sixth month.
  • $8,333 after the twelfth month.
  • $8,333 after the eighteenth month and before graduation.

Federal financial aid is not available because PRPA is a private program and not part of a university or college.

Request Admissions Information

Graduation requirements include

  • Complete all coursework and clinical rotations.
  • Maintain a minimum C average and overall GPA of 2.75.
  • Perform at least 75 satisfactory adult clinical bypass procedures.
  • Complete final written and clinical simulation examinations.
  • Current BLS certification.
  • Medical insurance and physical examination before matriculation.
  • Current immunizations and annual tuberculosis screening.
  • Compliance with drug-free workplace policies of clinical training sites.
